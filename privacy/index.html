Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Introduction

AmericanSaveAuto LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using our service, including:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Vehicle information (make, model, year, VIN)
  • Driving history and record information
  • Insurance history and current coverage details
  • Demographic information (age, gender, marital status)
  • Financial information for insurance qualification

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • IP address and geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referral source (how you found our site)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Third-Party Information

We may receive additional information about you from:

  • Public databases and motor vehicle records
  • Insurance partners for verification purposes
  • Credit reporting agencies (with your consent)
  • Social media platforms (if you connect your accounts)

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

Primary Services

  • Providing insurance quotes and comparisons
  • Connecting you with insurance providers
  • Processing and fulfilling your requests
  • Customer service and support

Communication

  • Sending insurance quotes and recommendations
  • Following up on your requests
  • Providing customer service communications
  • Sending promotional materials (with consent)

Website Improvement

  • Analyzing website usage and performance
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Improving our services and features
  • Conducting research and analytics

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Insurance Partners

We share your information with insurance companies and agents to:

  • Provide you with accurate quotes
  • Process insurance applications
  • Enable direct communication for coverage questions

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business:

  • Data processing and analytics companies
  • Customer service platforms
  • Email and communication services
  • Website hosting and security providers

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Protect the safety of users or the public

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and data storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and employee training
  • Industry-standard security protocols

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Communication Preferences

You can control communications by:

  • Using unsubscribe links in emails
  • Contacting us directly to update preferences
  • Adjusting settings in your account (if applicable)

State-Specific Rights

Residents of certain states may have additional rights:

  • California: Rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Virginia: Rights under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act
  • Other states: Rights as provided by applicable state privacy laws

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage
  • Personalize content and advertisements
  • Improve website functionality

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain business records as required

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. For material changes, we will provide notice through:

  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Prominent notices on our website
  • Other appropriate communication channels

International Users

Our services are primarily intended for users in the United States. If you are accessing our service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
